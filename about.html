Oliver Heron

Oliver is a pianist, improviser and composer living in London. His training in Jazz led him to be a semi-finalist for the BBC young musician competition in 2020, but since has directed his work towards the fields of non-idiomatic improvising and contemporary composition, studying the latter under Laurence Crane. His practice explores use of preparations and similarly derived sample playback to create an acousmatic blur of timbres, inspired by trailblazers such as Sophie Agnel, John Tilbury and Christian Wallumrød.
